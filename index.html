About SFC

Summer Fields Consulting was created to foster an environment for the millennial generation, providing them with valuable resources to improve as young professionals. Through business coaching and employment opportunity, Summer Fields Consulting is devoted to addressing key issues that may be experienced as a young professional, and working diligently to alleviate those issues to ensure everlasting success!

A Letter from the Founder

I’m not waiting for someone to pass me their baton. I’m running this race with my very own.

Briana McCullough

Founder

As a young entrepreneur, I fully understand the importance of wanting to have a direct and positive impact on the world. Many statistics on my generation will suggest that we want nothing more than to dedicate our unique talents to efforts that really matter. In times when our ideas are stifled due to our perceived lack of knowledge or experience, we are oftentimes left in a state of discouragement. With this in mind, I’ve chosen to create a platform to serve as an advocate to my generation. Entrepreneurship was not always something I wanted for myself. Choosing this route, however, has opened me up to an entirely new world! Now, I intend to use my own experiences, successes, and even failures to help other young professionals take charge of their careers. I want to cater to a generation who has constantly been overlooked, belittled, and turned away at the door just like I have. Working with my organization will not only encourage you to achieve new heights in your professional lives, but will also reaffirm that your ideas, your gifts, and your talents DO matter. I’m currently a student at Clemson University studying Sociology with an emphasis in Human Resources Management. I’m also a certified business coach with a passion for transforming businesses at risk of obsolescence into great “disrupters” of their industries! I look forward to connecting and providing sound solutions for all of your business needs!
King Regards, Briana McCullough;
